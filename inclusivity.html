The Interactive Guide

These panels are devoted to the interactive presentation of some further topics about the Olmec culture. Through this technological devices we introduce some notions about: the site of La Venta, religion related contents, glyphic writings, some other artefacts, and a brief overall presentation of the Mesoamerican civilizations that came after the Olmecs and of the contemporary civilisations which were spreading during the same time span but in other parts of the world.
