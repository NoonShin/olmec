A virtual museum

As core topic of our project we chose the seventeen colossal Olmec heads: one of the most impressive expressions of the pre-Columbian Mesoamerican art. Our goal is to imagine possible applications of the digital museology theories and tools aiming to facilitate an accessible but aware learning process of a very ancient and mysterious civilization through its artefacts.
